Creative / Product Designer / Technologist

Nick is an internationally awarded creative technologist, currently living in New York City and working as a Creative Director at Google.

Prior to Google, Nick co-founded New York-based technology studio Modern Assembly, and worked at B-Reel (both LA & NYC offices) as a creative technologist. Prior to B-Reel, Nick lead the development of interactive projects for the entertainment industry at the award winning studio, 65 Media in Los Angeles.

Currently, Nick is focused on building new products at Google, and on the side is tinkering with product design using the combination of natural materials and electronics.
